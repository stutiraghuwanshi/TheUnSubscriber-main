# The UnSubscriber

The UnSubscriber is a powerful and intuitive application designed to help you manage all your subscriptions in one place. Keep track of renewal dates, monitor your monthly and yearly spending, and receive timely reminders so you never miss a payment or an opportunity to unsubscribe.

## Features

- **Subscription Tracking:** Add, edit, and delete all your subscriptions with ease.
- **Spending Dashboard:** Get a clear overview of your monthly and yearly subscription costs.
- **Currency Conversion:** Switch between USD and INR to view your spending in your preferred currency.
- **AI-Powered Reminders:** Receive smart, personalized reminders for upcoming renewals via email or SMS.
- **Persistent Notifications:** Access all your active reminders anytime through the notification bell in the header.
- **Visual Spending Breakdown:** Understand where your money is going with an interactive bar chart.
- **Responsive Design:** Manage your subscriptions on any device, whether it's your desktop, tablet, or smartphone.
